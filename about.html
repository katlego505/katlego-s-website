About Naledi Motors

Built on passion, driven by excellence — discover the story behind Botswana's most trusted Mercedes-Benz dealership.

Est. 2026 — Francistown

The Meaning Behind the Name

In Setswana, Naledi means staran apt reflection of the three-pointed star that adorns every Mercedes-Benz. When our founder, Katlego Mokoya, opened our first showroom on Francistown Drive in 2026, the name was chosen deliberately: to represent aspiration, brilliance, and the enduring standards that define the Mercedes-Benz marvelous.

What began as a single showroom serving Francistown's discerning motorists has grown into one of Botswana's most respected luxury automotive destinations. Over 18 years, we have delivered more than 2,400 vehicles, maintained a 98% client satisfaction rating, and earned seven consecutive industry excellence awards — a record matched by few in the sector.

We are more than a dealership. We are custodians of a brand that has defined automotive luxury for over a century, and we take that responsibility seriously in every interaction, every service, every sale.
